Database Architect

Education Required: Database administrators and database architects usually have a bachelor's degree in a computer-related field, though some positions require a master's degree. New job opportunities are very likely in the future.

The average salary for a database in the United States is around $90,052 per year.

Employment of database administrators and architects is projected to grow 8 percent from 2020 to 2030, as fast as the average for all occupations. About 13,900 openings for database administrators and architects are projected each year, on average, over the decade.

Artificial Intelligence Specialists in America make an average salary of $162,918 per year or $78 per hour. The top 10 percent makes over $239,000 per year, while the bottom 10 percent under $110,000 per year.
